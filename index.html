My Heart's Story
This is all my feelings,every thought, every moment, every piece of my heart. These words carry the essence of us, of what we've shared and what we still feel, even when we’re apart. It's not just an expression of love! It’s a reflection of every moment we’ve spent together, the laughter, the touches, the quiet times, and even the struggles. It’s the story of how our love has grown, how it has blossomed in ways I never imagined, and how, even in silence or distance, it remains strong. It’s for the times we’ve sat together, talked, or simply been in each other’s presence, finding peace in the simple act of being close. It’s for the moments when we were apart, and I dreamed of you, feeling both the warmth and the longing in my heart. Every line holds the weight of my love for you! The kind of love that stays even in the toughest moments, the kind that doesn’t fade. Even when doubts or fear try to pull us away, know that my heart remains with you, holding on to everything we’ve built. This is our love, captured in words, a love that’s alive in my dreams, in my waking moments, and in everything I do. I hope you feel it, I hope you know it, and I hope you understand that no matter where we go from here, this love will always be a part of me, and a part of us.
